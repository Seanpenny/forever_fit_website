### Option 1: Netlify (EASIEST - Drag & Drop!)
1. Go to https://www.netlify.com
2. Sign up for free (use email or GitHub)
3. Drag your entire website folder onto the Netlify dashboard
4. Done! Your site is live instantly
5. You'll get a free URL like: `your-site-name.netlify.app`
6. Can add custom domain later for free

**Pros:** 
- No command line needed
- Free SSL certificate
- Custom domain support
- Automatic deployments
- Fast CDN

---

### Option 2: Vercel (Also Very Easy)
1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Sign up for free
3. Click "Add New Project"
4. Drag and drop your folder OR connect via command line
5. Your site goes live instantly

**Pros:**
- Super fast
- Free SSL
- Custom domains
- Great performance

---

### Option 3: Cloudflare Pages
1. Go to https://pages.cloudflare.com
2. Sign up (free)
3. Upload your folder
4. Get instant free hosting

**Pros:**
- Free SSL
- Fast global CDN
- Custom domains
- No bandwidth limits
