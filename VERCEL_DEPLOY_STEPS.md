# Vercel Deployment - 10 Steps (10 Words Each)

## Step 1
Click "Add New Project" button on your Vercel dashboard.

## Step 2
Choose "Import Git Repository" or "Deploy" button option.

## Step 3
If importing from GitHub, select your forever-_website repository now.

## Step 4
Or click "Browse" to upload your website folder directly instead.

## Step 5
Select your entire "Forever fit website" folder from your computer.

## Step 6
Vercel will detect your index.html file automatically for you.

## Step 7
Click "Deploy" button and wait for build process to complete.

## Step 8
Your site will be live at a URL like yourname.vercel.app.

## Step 9
Copy your new website URL and share it with others.

## Step 10
Done! Your Forever Fit website is now live and free.
